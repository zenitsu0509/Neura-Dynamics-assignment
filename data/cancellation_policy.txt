**Cancellation Policy**

**1. Order Cancellation**
- You may cancel your order within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund, provided the order has not yet shipped.
- To cancel an order, please email cancellations@example.com with your order number.

**2. Cancellation Fees**
- Orders cancelled after 24 hours but before shipping may be subject to a 10% restocking fee.
- Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You must wait to receive the item and then follow our Refund Policy.

**3. Subscription Cancellations**
- For subscription services, you may cancel at any time.
- Cancellations made at least 3 days before the next billing cycle will prevent the next charge.
- No partial refunds are given for unused portions of a subscription term.
