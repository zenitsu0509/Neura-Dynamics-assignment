**Shipping Policy**

**1. Shipping Methods and Costs**
- We offer Standard (5-7 business days), Expedited (2-3 business days), and Overnight shipping.
- Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
- Free Standard shipping is available for orders over $50 within the continental US.

**2. Processing Time**
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
- Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

**3. International Shipping**
- We currently ship to Canada, UK, and Australia.
- International customers are responsible for any customs, duties, or taxes imposed by their country.

**4. Tracking**
- Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number.
- Please allow 24 hours for the tracking information to update.
